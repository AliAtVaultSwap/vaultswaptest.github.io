Making your job easier
 
 
The need for simultaneous file exchange arises in many areas, including during litigation, during negotiation, and during the course of discovery.
Often, these exchanges are done physically, in person to ensure security. Traditionally, email has been another platform used to make such exchanges.
This is problematic for two reasons....
 
 
 

Trust

The assumption made in an asychronous exchange like email is that you trust the other party to send their file. However, email and other current file sharing platforms do not guarantee such an exchange nor does it adequately document misconduct or malfeasance

Convenience

Currently, to attempt a truly synchronized exchange both parties would have to coordinate a time that suits everyone's schedules, arrange a meeting or platform through which the exchange occurs, and wait to physically send each other the documents. Our platform avoids all of this by making the files available at the decided time when both parties have uploaded their documents.

Security

Dropbox, google drive, email, or any other file repository does not guarantee confidentiality of clients' documents. These platforms do not guarantee that the opposing party has exclusive viewing privilege. A vault on our platform is an ad hoc exchange between two parties that encrypts all files using a 256-bit encryption algorithm. This ensures the confidentiality of the exchange even from us.
